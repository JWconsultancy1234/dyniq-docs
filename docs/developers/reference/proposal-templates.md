## Proposal Structure

1. **Problem Statement** - Their pain
2. **Dream Outcome** - What they want
3. **Our Solution** - How we deliver
4. **Proof** - Case studies
5. **Offer Stack** - Everything included
6. **Investment** - Price with context
7. **Guarantee** - Risk reversal
8. **Next Steps** - Clear CTA
